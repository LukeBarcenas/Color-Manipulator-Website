What are channel swapped colors?

Channel swapped colors are colors that manipulate the RGB order of your chosen color. On this site, 5 different combinations of the colors you choose are shown, RBG, GRB, GBR, BRG, and BGR. These colors could be vastly different or identical to your chosen color based on how you make it.

About the Color Manipulator

The Color Manipulator was an experimental color test that I wanted to conduct to see how different RGB values look and interact. I find the idea of channel swapped colors very interesting, as they could represent colors on complete different sides of the color wheel. Though they are not complimentary, some can make for very interesting color combinations. Exploring these interesting combinations is what this website is for.
